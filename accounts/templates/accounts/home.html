Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about The Handymen

When you make a payment, the funds are held in our secure M-Pesa Escrow account. The artisan is notified that payment is ready, but they don't receive a single shilling until YOU confirm the job is complete. You have 48 hours to inspect the work - if you're not satisfied, we help mediate and can return your funds.

Every artisan undergoes a rigorous verification process: we check their government-issued ID, review their certificates and training documents, call their references, and conduct a short interview. Only after passing all these steps can they join our platform.

We primarily use M-Pesa for all transactions - it's fast, secure, and accessible to all Kenyans. Simply send the agreed amount to our paybill number, and we'll hold it in escrow until the job is complete.

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to help resolve any disputes. Since funds are held in escrow, we can mediate and ensure a fair outcome. Plus, all work comes with a 7-day warranty - if issues arise within a week, we'll help make it right.
